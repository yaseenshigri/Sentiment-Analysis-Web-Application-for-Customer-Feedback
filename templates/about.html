About Us

Welcome to the Sentiment Analysis Web App. This application allows you to analyze the sentiment of any text you input. It uses a machine learning model trained to recognize positive, neutral, and negative sentiments.

Our goal is to help individuals and businesses understand the emotional tone of their text data, whether it's customer feedback, social media posts, or any other form of text.
